Welcome.

Field Notes is a notebook of signals and traces gathered along the way.

Some entries are observations. Some become stories. Some remain fragments. Together they form a record of attention: things noticed, explored, built, read, listened to, and remembered.

### Signals & Traces

The notebook itself.

A place for observations before they disappear.

### The Library

Beyond the crystal doors of the Vault of Grayskull lies the archive: books, comics, manuals, field guides, and other collected artifacts that have traveled with me over the years.

### Archives
