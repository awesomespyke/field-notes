## The Frame

I recently set up a shared digital picture frame that I've started calling **The Frame**.

The idea is simple: if something catches your eye and makes you think, "Spyke would appreciate this," I'd love to see it too.

It might be a beautiful landscape, an interesting object, your latest project, a rainy afternoon, your pets, your travels, or simply an ordinary moment that felt worth noticing.

If you'd like to contribute, just mention The Frame to me and I'll send you an invitation through Aura Frames.

Unlike a social feed, nothing here is selected by an algorithm. Every image appears on my desk because someone chose to share it. Each one is a small glimpse into another person's world—a sight offered from one observer to another.
